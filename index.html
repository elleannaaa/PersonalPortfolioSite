Hello there! I'm Chloe Stricker, in the final chapter of my Peace Studies major, sprinkled with the enriching experiences of Chapman University's Honors Program. My journey began in Seattle, WA, leading me first to the vibrant heart of Orange County. There, amidst the academic halls of Chapman, I found my second home. Eventually, my path meandered to the bustling streets of Los Angeles, a city teeming with opportunities and adventures that have since become a significant part of my narrative. Along this journey of exploration and self-discovery, two joys have remained constant: my profound affection for the serene company of cats and my unwavering love for the spirited zest of matcha. Step into my world, where academia intertwines with personal passions, painting a life rich with experiences and vibrant encounters.
